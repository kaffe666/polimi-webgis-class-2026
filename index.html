About This Project

01

Data Collection

CAMS reanalysis rasters, ESRI Land Cover, WorldPop population grid and GAUL Level 2 boundaries.

02

Spatial Analysis

Clipping, reprojection, annual averaging, reclassification and zonal statistics in QGIS.

03

Exposure Assessment

5×5 bivariate mapping of pollutant concentration classes against population density by administrative zone.

Key Findings

NO₂ peaks near Athens and northern urban corridors; PM₁₀ is highest in northern lowlands and construction zones; PM₂.₅ is concentrated in the Athens basin. Population-weighted exposure reveals that high-risk zones are smaller in area but affect a disproportionately large share of the population.
